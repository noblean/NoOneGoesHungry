About Us

No One Goes Hungry (NOGH) started back on Sunday Nov 1st, 2020, at Caribbean Vibes Milton where we cooked 300 meals and fed our community with the awesome volunteers like Kimberley Savage-Paulson and her son, Maliha Khan Ahmed and so many more. Within a month the Canadian Caribbean Association of Halton helped us thru funding and coordination of Oakville volunteers, families to feed and so much more to expand the program to Oakville on a Saturday and one month after that we started NOGH in Acton and Georgetown where our amazing volunteers deliver from Milton to the seniors and other families who sign up.
